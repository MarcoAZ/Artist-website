"NATURE"

My interest in nature started at an early age when I habitually watched Animal Planet and nature documentaries. My parents were doctors and my first close encounters with animals were in a lab. I believe this early interaction shaped how I viewed the natural world around me. I am fascinated in how people interact with and think they interact with “nature” in general.

My focus has mostly been works on paper. It was a very easy decision for me to combine drawing techniques with the idea of science and nature. I chose pen and ink for my medium because I felt it put the images on an equal level. I am drawing animals and their toy counterparts and thought that the extravagant color of the toys would be too stark of a contrast. I wanted the idea of “real” nature versus an idea of nature constructed by humans to be more understated.

Daniella is an artist and freelance designer located in Little Rock, AR. She is available for commissioned art and freelance design work. She studied Studio Art and Graphic Design at American University in Washington, DC.
