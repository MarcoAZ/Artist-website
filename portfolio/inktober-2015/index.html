INKTOBER 2015

During the month of October I challenged myself to draw one ink illustration per day. Using micron pens and Prismacolor markers, I focused on a single subject rather than a full composition. This allowed me to explore the media and go into detail without spending too much time on each drawing.

Daniella is an artist and freelance designer located in Little Rock, AR. She is available for commissioned art and freelance design work. She studied Studio Art and Graphic Design at American University in Washington, DC.
