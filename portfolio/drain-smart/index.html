DRAIN SMART

Drain Smart is an engaging public educational/environmental program that uses local artists' original murals on storm drains to educate the public about runoff water and the effects on local water resources, like Fourche Creek and the Arkansas River. My mural "Sunset Herons" won first place in the Downtown/River Market category. For more information on the Drain Smart program visit drain-smart.org

My interest in nature started at an early age in Florida where you were just as likely to spot an alligator in a pond as you were a duck. Growing up in Arkansas only strengthened that interest. I still love spotting birds and other animals in the creeks around Little Rock. Great Blue Herons have always been one of my favorite birds to see. They are so big you can’t help but stare. I hope by using them in my design that people will stop, look at the drain, and become more aware about where the water flows. I wanted to focus on animals that are native to Arkansas because they are the ones being affected by our pollution.

Daniella is an artist and freelance designer located in Little Rock, AR. She is available for commissioned art and freelance design work. She studied Studio Art and Graphic Design at American University in Washington, DC.
