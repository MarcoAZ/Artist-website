GUIDE TO THE SAINTS

During my semester abroad in Italy I visited a lot of cathedrals and learned about local saints. I chose the most interesting stories and illustrated a set of cards with their names and stories. I constructed a clam-shell box to house the cards.

Daniella is an artist and freelance designer located in Little Rock, AR. She is available for commissioned art and freelance design work. She studied Studio Art and Graphic Design at American University in Washington, DC.
