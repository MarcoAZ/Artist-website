PRINTMAKING

I see printmaking as the perfect combination of fine art, illustration, and design. Like many of my other works, I find my inspiration in nature. Most recently, I’ve been drawn to linocut because of the ease of translating an idea to the block. Other techniques I’ve used include: woodcut, intaglio etching, and drypoint etching.

Daniella is an artist and freelance designer located in Little Rock, AR. She is available for commissioned art and freelance design work. She studied Studio Art and Graphic Design at American University in Washington, DC.
